A Little About Me

Once upon a time, I was working as an administrative assistant in an office. I always seemed to find ways to play around with coding. Once, I taught myself how to write custom functions for Google Sheets to streamline the office supply inventory. I realized that I had a real passion for writing code.

Going back to school and pursuing a career as a software engineer seemed like a fantasy, but I decided to jump in and try.

I am currently working toward a B.S. in Computer Information Systems at Cal Poly Pomona. I’ve discovered that my previous jobs in retail and as an admin assistant taught me the importance of hard work and collaboration. And I’ve carried that same attitude into my studies.

My curiosity about all things tech and passion for coding have driven me to explore projects outside the classroom. I just finished a fun full stack web project that you can check out here.

I’m always looking for new challenges. I enjoy solving problems and learning from the people around me. It’s been an amazing journey so far!
